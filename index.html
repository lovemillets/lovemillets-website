A Sincere Philosophy

Let's Decode the "Healthy Food" Lies

A lot of packaged food claims to be "healthy" on the front, but hides cheap wheat fillers, artificial binders, and processed sugar on the back. At Love Millets, we practice **front-of-pack truth**.

Standard "Healthy" Flour & Mixes

The Hidden Reality

  • Diluted with Wheat: Often labeled "Ragi" or "Millet" flour but mixed with cheap refined wheat flour (Maida) to cut costs.
  • Chemical Binders: Artificial gums and emulsifiers added to improve dough texture because millets are naturally gluten-free.
  • Sneaky Sugars: Maltodextrin, corn syrup, or refined sugar added to breakfast premixes to enhance flavor, spike energy, and increase shelf-life.

Love Millets Clean Promise

Our Unshakable Truth

  • 100% Pure Grains: What you see is what you get. Our Ragi flour contains exactly 100% Ragi—completely unadulterated.
  • Zero Binders or Chemicals: No stabilizers, thickeners, or synthetic gums. We celebrate the natural gluten-free characteristics of millets.
  • Absolutely No Maltodextrin: Our products contain zero refined sugar or artificial sweeteners. Any future sweetness will come purely from natural whole fruits (like dates) or natural honey, keeping our core range completely unsweetened.
